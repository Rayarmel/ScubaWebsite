How to start your scuba diving journey

  1. First and foremost you must speak with your doctor
    • This is very important as there are some health conditions that can make the diving experience very unsafe.
    • BE SURE TO SPEAK WITH A HEALTH CARE PROFESSIONAL!!
  2. After getting in contact with a doctor, your next step is to find a course
    • Scuba Diving courses are essential in the way that they help you to truly understand what you are doing underwater.
    • Tryning to go underwater diving without training is HIGHLY DANGEROUS! Never attempt this as there are plenty of contigencies that you will not be ready for.
  3. Once you are certified and a liscensed diver, your next step would be to purchase some diving gear. Some gear that you would want to absolutely get include:
    • Scuba mask
    • Fins
    • Snorkel
    • Regulator
    • Wetsuit
    • Diving computer
